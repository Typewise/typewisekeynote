Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to our most commonly asked questions

What are your delivery timeframes?

Standard delivery is completed within 3-5 business days. Express delivery options are available for next-day delivery in most metropolitan areas.

What is your return and refund policy?

We provide a 30-day return window for most products. Items must be returned in original condition with complete packaging and all included accessories.

Do you offer competitive price matching?

Yes, we provide price matching for authorized retailers. Submit the competitor's pricing details and we will match the offer.

How can I track my order status?

A tracking number will be sent to your email upon shipment. Use this number on our order tracking page or the carrier's website for real-time updates.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Flexible financing options are available for purchases exceeding $500.
